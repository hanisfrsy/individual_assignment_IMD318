Hello! My name is Nur Farisya Hanis Binti Azhar and you can call me Farisya. I was born on the 15th of February 2004 at Hospital Kajang. I have always been drawn to vibrant and calming colors with purple being my absolute favorite. In my free time, I love playing with my cat since it’s one of my favorite ways to unwind.

Currently, I am pursuing my Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Machang, and I’m proud to be in my final semester. University life has been an eye-opening experience for me since it teach me so many things I never knew before, from academic knowledge to real-world skills and self-discovery.

I’m grateful for the opportunities I’ve had to grow and develop my skills and I look forward to applying them in my future endeavors. Thanks for visiting my website! I hope you enjoy exploring it and learning more about my academic journey and personal experiences.
